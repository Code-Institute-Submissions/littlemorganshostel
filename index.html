Welcome

Looking for some chill out time in an idyllic setting?

You are in the right place! Come relax in our treehouses spread over a six acre property, filled with flowers, mango trees, coconut palm trees and animals (dogs, cats, pigs, chickens, ducks and the occasional monkey), have a swim in the lake located a few meters from your room, or enjoy a beautiful sunset above both Ometepe volcanoes in one of our two miradors.

Take a look at what the island is like!

Check out our facilities!

BAR

We are known around the island to be one of the best spots to hang out at our spacious open bar : always loaded with great tunes, it is the perfect place to party and meet fellow travelers, try our homemade Baileys and Sambuca, play pool or any of our multiple games, or watch sport games with a cold beer.

HOSTEL

We have a number of different dorms and private rooms : 3 treehouse dorms with breathtaking views of the lake and volcanoes as well as 7 cabanas, all with a little terrace and hammocks. Our biggest dorm features 6 beds and we have privates that sleeps up to three people. And as we know it is hard to leave Little Morgan's your 4th night will be free!
