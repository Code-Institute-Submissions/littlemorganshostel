Welcome

Looking for some chill out time in an idyllic setting?

You are in the right place! Come relax in our treehouses spread over a six acre property, filled with flowers, mango trees, coconut palm trees and animals (dogs, cats, pigs, chickens, ducks and the occasional monkey), have a swim in the lake located a few meters from your room, or enjoy a beautiful sunset above both Ometepe volcanoes in one of our two miradors.
